## My preferences

- mature
- currently maintained
- works for JavaScript ES6+
- works from the Linux command line or Visual Studio Code IDE

## Tools

1. :thumbsup: [GitHub: terser / terser](https://github.com/terser/terser)
   - Seems to meet all my preferences | Have installed and seems to perform well on Linux command line

1. [npmx | oxc-minify](https://npmx.dev/package/oxc-minify)
   - At June 2026 | Still new 0.136.0 | Looks promising

1. [GitHub: ndparker / rjsmin](https://github.com/ndparker/rjsmin)
   - At June 2026 | No updates for 8 months | Not sure if it supports ES6+ or not

1. [GitHub: mishoo / UglifyJS](https://github.com/mishoo/UglifyJS)
   - At June 2026 | No updates for 18 months | Does not support ES6+

[rspivak's slimit](https://github.com/rspivak/slimit) and [fizker's minifier](https://github.com/fizker/minifier)
have not been maintained for 8-10 years. I suspect neither support ES6+.
